Common Questions.

What do I need to provide?

Just your listing URL or a shared folder of your best property photos. We take it from there — no briefs, no prep work, no back-and-forth required.

Do you post directly to my accounts, or hand me files?

It depends on your tier. The Seedling delivers ready-to-post files each month — you control the posting. The Homestead and Acreage tiers include full account management; we post directly to your Instagram and Facebook on your schedule.

Which platforms do you manage?

Instagram and Facebook are our primary focus — that's where rural buyers and land investors spend their time. The Acreage tier adds paid ad management across both platforms.

Do I get to approve content before it goes live?

Yes, always. Every piece of content goes through an approval step before anything is scheduled or delivered. One round of revisions is included at no charge.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All tiers are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. We earn your business through results, not paperwork.

How quickly can I get started?

Most clients are fully onboarded within 3 business days. After that, new listing content is turned around within 48 hours of receiving your assets.
