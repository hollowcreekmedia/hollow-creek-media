**Last Updated:** May 2026  
**Status:** Active (No clients yet — preparing for launch)

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Hollow Creek Media is a done-for-you social media management service built specifically for rural real estate agents. The business model is built on:

1. **Monthly content creation** (clients submit on their anniversary date, you deliver 5 days later)
2. **Three tiered pricing** (Sprout $349, Grove $699, Acreage $1,299)
3. **Sustainable workload** (3-4 hours per client per month, spread throughout the month)
4. **Land-focused positioning** (not generic real estate templates)

---

## THE THREE PRICING TIERS

### TIER 1: SPROUT ($349/month)

**Best For:** Agents testing social media, tight budgets, agents building baseline presence

**What They Get:**
- 4 posts/month (1 per week)
- Instagram only
- Hashtag research (monthly)
- All content delivered as ready-to-post files (agent posts themselves)
- 5-day delivery from submission
- Email support
- Month-to-month, no contract

**What You Do:**
- Write simple captions (4 posts/month)
- Design basic graphics (4 graphics/month)
- Research hashtags
- Create simple content calendar
- Per-client time: ~1-2 hours/month

**Conversion Path:** Sprout agents typically upgrade to Grove after seeing results in 2-3 months.

---

### TIER 2: GROVE ($699/month)

**Best For:** Active agents, established presence, agents with consistent deals

**What They Get:**
- 8 posts/month (2 per week)
- Instagram + Facebook (platform-optimized)
- 1 Reel per month (15-30 second short-form video)
- 1 email campaign per month
- Full account management (we post directly)
- Monthly content calendar
- Slack support (24-hour response)
- 5-day delivery from submission
- Month-to-month, no contract

**What You Do:**
- Write 8 captions per month (adapted per platform)
- Design 8 graphics per month
- Create/edit 1 Reel per month (script → film → edit)
- Write 1 email campaign
- Manage posting schedule
- Per-client time: ~3-4 hours/month

**Why Grove is the revenue driver:** Most agents will land here or upgrade from Sprout. The Reel inclusion shows immediate engagement boost. This is your most profitable tier.

---

### TIER 3: ACREAGE ($1,299/month)

**Best For:** High-volume agents, serious operators, agents wanting competitive edge

**What They Get:**
- 16 posts/month (4 per week across all platforms)
- All three platforms (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
- 4 Reels/month (1 per week, TikTok-native content)
- 2 email campaigns/month
- Full account management + posting
- Native TikTok content (not reposts from IG)
- Quarterly market analysis + competitor tracking
- Personal account manager (monthly 30-min strategy call)
- Performance dashboard + monthly metrics
- Priority support (24-hour guaranteed response)
- 5-day delivery from submission
- Month-to-month, no contract

**What You Do:**
- Write 16 captions per month (platform-specific)
- Design 16 graphics (platform-optimized)
- Create/edit 4 Reels (2-3 hours each = 8-12 hours video work)
- Write 2 email campaigns
- Manage all three platforms
- Conduct quarterly market analysis
- Monthly strategy calls
- Per-client time: ~8-10 hours/month

**Why Acreage justifies $1,299:** TikTok is where rural agent competition is weakest. Agents at this tier see 6-10x ROI vs. Sprout tier. Account manager + quarterly reports are enterprise-level service.

## THE SUBMISSION WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Client Onboarding

**Timeline:** When client commits to a tier

**What You Send Them:**
- Google Form with 5 questions:
  1. What platforms are you currently on?
  2. What are your brand colors / do you have a logo?
  3. What types of properties do you sell most?
  4. Who is your ideal buyer/seller?
  5. Do you want to post yourself or give us login access?

**Action:** Set their submission date based on their preference (10th, 15th, 22nd, etc.)

---

### Step 2: Monthly Submission

**Timeline:** On their chosen date (e.g., 15th of each month)

**What Client Sends You:**
- Email or shared folder containing:
  - New listing photos (property images)
  - Listing URL or details (price, acreage, location, special features)
  - Any updates from their month (sold properties, price reductions, offers, closings)
  - Any specific stories or news they want highlighted

**What They DON'T Need to Do:**
- Write captions
- Design graphics
- Research hashtags
- Think about scheduling
- Plan content strategy

---

### Step 3: Content Creation (Days 1-5 After Submission)

**Your Timeline:**

**Day 1-2: Captions & Strategy**
- Write all captions (4-16 depending on tier)
- Optimize for each platform
- Include hashtags and engagement hooks
- Create content calendar (when to post each piece)

**Day 2-3: Design & Graphics**
- Design all graphics in Canva
- Ensure platform-specific dimensions
- Create Reel scripts (if Acreage tier)

**Day 3-4: Video Production (if applicable)**
- Shoot Reels (if you have video equipment)
- Edit Reels in CapCut, Adobe Premiere, or similar
- Export for each platform

**Day 5: Final Delivery**
- Compile all assets
- Send client approval package
- Get approval (max 1 round of revisions included)

---

### Step 4: Posting

**Sprout Tier:**
- You send ZIP file with all images + captions doc
- Client posts themselves on their schedule

**Grove/Acreage Tier:**
- You post directly to their accounts (they give you login access)
- OR you use Buffer/Later to schedule for their preferred posting times
- Post on consistent schedule (e.g., Tuesdays/Thursdays at 9am)

---

### Step 5: Monthly Report

**Timeline:** End of month

**What You Send:**
- Screenshot of analytics (reach, engagement, followers gained, top-performing post)
- Simple summary of the month's performance
- Note which content types resonated best

## YOUR MONTHLY WORKLOAD DISTRIBUTION

**Scenario: 10 Grove-tier clients**

- 10 clients × 8 posts/client = 80 posts/month
- 10 clients × 1 Reel/client = 10 Reels/month
- Total per-client time: 3-4 hours × 10 clients = 30-40 hours/month
- Spread across the month (not clustered)

**Daily/Weekly Rhythm:**

- **Days 1-9:** Client onboarding + submissions coming in
- **Days 5-14:** Creating content for first week's submissions
- **Days 10-19:** New submissions arriving + creating for previous submissions
- **Days 15-24:** Same pattern continues
- **Days 20-29:** Same pattern continues
- **Days 25-30:** Wrapping up stragglers + monthly reports

**Result:** Steady, predictable workload throughout the month. No burnout sprints. Better work quality.

## PRICING JUSTIFICATION

### Why These Price Points?

**Sprout at $349:**
- Single platform = 40% less work than multi-platform
- No video = no production overhead
- Competitive with budget agencies ($99-500 range)
- Shows quality without high commitment

**Grove at $699:**
- Two platforms = moderate complexity
- 1 Reel/month = manageable video workload
- Account management + full scheduling = real value-add
- Per-post cost: $87 (still efficient)
- This is industry standard for this tier

**Acreage at $1,299:**
- Three platforms with native content = significant work
- 4 Reels/month = substantial video production
- Account manager + strategy calls = enterprise service
- TikTok included = competitive advantage
- Per-post cost: $73 (economy of scale)
- Market comparable: $3,500-5,000 for this level
- You're 30-40% below market = attractive positioning

### Market Benchmarks You're Operating In

- Small business social media average: $800-$2,500/month
- Real estate social media average: $1,500-$3,500/month
- Premium real estate services: $3,500-$8,000+/month
- Your Acreage tier ($1,299) = compelling value
