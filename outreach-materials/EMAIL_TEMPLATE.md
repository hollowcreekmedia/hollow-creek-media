## SENDING BEST PRACTICES

**Timing:**
- Tuesday-Thursday (best response days)
- 10am-2pm local agent time
- Avoid early morning or late evening

**Follow-Up Schedule:**
- Initial email: Day 1
- Follow-up #1: Day 7 (different angle, second property)
- Follow-up #2: Day 14 (value-focused, no pressure)
- Then: Move to next prospect or try LinkedIn/phone

**Subject Line Tips:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use agent's property name when possible
- Create curiosity without being clickbait
- Avoid all caps or multiple exclamation marks

**Call-to-Action Tips:**
- Ask for 15 minutes (not an hour)
- Offer specific value (not vague "chat")
- Make it easy to say yes (calendly link, direct phone)
- No pressure framing (optional "no worries if not")
