## OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Email Subject Line Options
- "3 Social Posts We'd Create for Your [Property Name]"
- "See What Your Listings Could Look Like — 48-Hour Social Suite"
- "Your Hunting Land Deserves Better Marketing — Sample Posts Enclosed"
- "Professional Social Content — 48 Hours, Zero Hassle"

### Email Body Template
See EMAIL_TEMPLATE.md

### Contact Best Practices
1. **Timing:** Send between 10am-2pm local agent time
2. **Personalization:** Always reference a specific current listing
3. **Social Proof:** Include one sample post that matches their property type exactly
4. **Clear CTA:** Offer a 15-minute discovery call, not a sales pitch
5. **Follow-up:** 5-7 days if no response

### Success Metrics
- **Expected Open Rate:** 25-35% (with personalization)
- **Expected Response Rate:** 10-20% (with sample posts)
- **Average Conversion:** 15-25% of responders become clients
- **Lead Quality:** Very high (pre-qualified by property type and market)
