## USAGE GUIDELINES

**When Sending to Agents:**
1. Select the 3 posts that best match THEIR most recent listing
2. Use THEIR actual property photos (request from Zillow, Realtor.com, or their site)
3. Replace [LINK] with direct link to their property listing
4. Format for the agent's specific platform strength (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
5. Include note: "These are samples of what we'd create—every week, for every listing"

**Customization:**
- Add agent's name/handle to posts where appropriate
- Insert specific property names instead of generic descriptions
- Reference local market trends specific to their region
- Use their property photos, not stock images

**Best Performing Post Types:**
1. Property Showcase (highest engagement for land)
2. Behind-the-Scenes/Local Knowledge (builds authority)
3. Education/Market Insights (drives long-term credibility)
